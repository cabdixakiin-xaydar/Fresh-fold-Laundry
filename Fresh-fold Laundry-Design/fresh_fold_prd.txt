# Fresh-Fold Laundry Management System - Requirements Specification

## 1. Overview
Automate and streamline laundry business operations, including customers, orders, billing, inventory, and employees.

## 2. Customer Management
- Register, update, and remove customers.
- Store contact details (name, phone, address).
- Transaction history and preferences.
- Loyalty programs and discounts.

## 3. Order Management
- Unique order IDs.
- Multiple items per order.
- Service types: Washing, Dry Cleaning, Ironing.
- Special instructions (delicate fabrics, stain removal).
- Real-time status: Received, Processing, Ready, Delivered.

## 4. Pricing and Billing
- Charges by item count or weight.
- Automatic total calculation.
- Invoices/receipts generation.
- Supports discounts, taxes, and promos.
- Payment status tracking (Paid, Partial, Unpaid).

## 5. Inventory Management
- Track supplies (detergents, chemicals).
- Low stock alerts.
- Supplier/vendor management.

## 6. Employee Management
- Staff accounts and roles (Admin, Cashier, Worker).
- Performance tracking, attendance, and shift schedules.

## 7. Pickup & Delivery
- Scheduling, driver assignment, and status tracking.
- Route optimization.

## 8. Reporting & Analytics
- Sales reports (Daily, Weekly, Monthly).
- Customer activity and financial health tracking (Profit/Expenses).
- Service popularity insights.

## 9. Security
- Secure login and Role-Based Access Control (RBAC).
- Data encryption and backups.

## 10. Dashboard
- Centralized overview of KPIs (Total orders, revenue, pending tasks).
- Quick actions for common operations.
